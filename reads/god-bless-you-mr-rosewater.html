Now that's the thing with Kurt Vonnegut, it takes patience to be able to enjoy his brilliance. In the first reading you might think, this is not making any sense. I can hardly explain the plot of this book to my friends. You might even start questioning your sanity and your reading skills. But don't worry, that's okay, you are not completely useless or insane, and even if you are then know that this book is just trying to tell that even useless people can be loved.
