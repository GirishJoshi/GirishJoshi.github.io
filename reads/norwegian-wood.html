Review
Norwegian Wood is a song by The Beatles which was about a boy who spends a night with a girl in her room, sitting on her rug, talking, and drinking wine. When he wakes up in the morning, he finds himself alone, she's gone. I think this book's similar to that song. "no truth can cure the sadness we feel from losing a loved one. No truth, no sincerity, no strength, no kindness, can cure that sorrow. All we can do is see that sadness through to the end and learn something from it, but what we learn will be no help in facing the next sadness that comes to us without warning."
This book leaves me feeling hauntingly sad. It was beautifully written, Murakami's words flow like a gentle river.
