Review
In the junior year of my college (almost three years now), we organized an event on this theme: The Digital Transformation of the Oil and Gas Industry. I had spent a year or so learning digital technologies like Data Analytics and IoT myself, and I just wanted everyone in my college to learn about them too. Back then I was highly fascinated with this trend, but I always lacked words to spread my enthusiasm about it. In fact, I was asked to explain what I meant by this during my job interviews, but I fumbled and failed. It was always so hard to explain how digital technologies are engaging in Oil & Gas Industry to the recent graduates, let alone veterans of the industry. This book gave me words to express myself. It's filled with case studies and insights, for me, it was a page-turner. However, I felt that if you are not familiar with digital technologies and terms then you might struggle a bit.
